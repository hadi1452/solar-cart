Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know About Solar Energy

What Is Solar Cart?+

Solar Cart Is An Online Store For iTel Solar Products In Karachi. We Sell Solar Panels, Hybrid Inverters, Lithium Batteries And Complete Energy Storage Solutions.

How Does Solar Energy Work?+

Solar Panels Convert Sunlight Into Electricity. A Hybrid Inverter Converts This DC Power Into AC Power For Your Home. Batteries Store Excess Energy For Use At Night Or During Load Shedding.

How Much Can I Save With Solar?+

Most Customers Save 70-100% On Their Electricity Bills. Use Our Solar Calculator To Get An Estimate Based On Your Current Bill.

Do You Offer Installation Services?+

We Can Connect You With Trusted Installation Partners In Karachi. Contact Us On WhatsApp For Installation Quotes.

What Brands Do You Sell?+

We Sell iTel Inverters & Batteries, Longi Solar Panels, And Jinko Solar Panels. All Are Tier-1 Brands With Official Warranties.

What Size Solar System Do I Need?+

It Depends On Your Monthly Electricity Bill. A 4kW System Works For Most Homes. Use Our Solar Calculator For A Personalized Recommendation.

What Is LiFePO4 Battery?+

LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) Is The Safest And Longest-Lasting Lithium Battery Technology. It Offers 6000+ Charge Cycles, 10+ Year Lifespan, And No Risk Of Fire Or Explosion.

What Is A Hybrid Inverter?+

A Hybrid Inverter Can Work With Both Solar Panels And Batteries. It Automatically Switches Between Solar, Battery, And Grid Power For Maximum Efficiency And Uninterrupted Supply.

Can I Run AC On Solar?+

Yes! A 4kW Or Higher Inverter Can Run 1-2 ACs Along With Other Appliances. The iTel 6kW And 8kW Inverters Are Popular Choices For Running Multiple ACs.

How Long Does Delivery Take?+

Can I Track My Order?+

Yes! Use Our Order Tracking Page. Enter Your Order ID (e.g. SC-20260601) To Check Your Order Status In Real-Time.

Solar Panels: 12 Year Product + 30 Year Performance Warranty. Inverters: 3-5 Years. Batteries: 5-10 Years. All Warranties Are Official From The Manufacturer.

Privacy Policy - Solar Cart

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

Solar Cart ("we", "our", "us") operates the website solar-cart-apvs.vercel.app and the Solar Cart mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect the following information:

- Full Name
- Phone Number
- City & Delivery Address
- Order Details (products, quantity, total amount)
- Payment Reference / Transaction ID

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

- Process and deliver your orders
- Contact you regarding order updates and delivery
- Provide customer support via WhatsApp and phone
- Improve our products and services
- Send promotional offers (only with your consent)

3. Data Storage

Your order data is stored securely. We use browser local storage for cart, wishlist, and preference data on your device. Order information is transmitted securely via WhatsApp and stored in our order management system.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- WhatsApp: For order communication and customer support
- Vercel: For website hosting
- Google Fonts: For typography
- Askari Bank: For payment processing

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your delivery details with our logistics partners solely for the purpose of delivering your order.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to save your shopping cart, wishlist, theme preference, and language selection. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Opt out of promotional communications
- Clear your local browser data at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
